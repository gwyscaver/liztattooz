The Artist

Rooted in tradition,
drawn by hand.

Liz creates fun, bold, clean American Traditional work that honors the roots of the craft. Adding a twist to the tradition, everything is hand-drawn with intention — thick outlines, saturated color, and imagery that stands the test of time.

Based in the Signature Studio (cats not yet permitted, despite repeated negotiations), Liz pulls from vintage editorial and old-school graphic design to give every piece a sharp, considered aesthetic with a personality all its own.

“A good tattoo should look like it was always supposed to be there.”
